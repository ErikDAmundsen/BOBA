Bubble teas in Mason jars by Ice-Pandora

Watch
Digital Art / Pixel Art / Objects / Isometric
#bubbletea #masonjar #miniature #pixel #pixelart #pixelfood #miniaturefood
Why hello, it's been a while! And I'm sorry for the long long hiatus here. (sorry for the re-upload, I was having a little thumbnail
situation here)

Recently I was feeling in making pixelart again so here I am with new art again: bubble tea in maison jars! 
Mason jars are quite popular table ware lately where lots of cafés and hipster people use them for drinking
and I happen to be inspired of.
Bubble teas are of course amazingly delicious too, if you have not tried it out yet please do! Out of curiosity,
what is your favorite tea? 

Software: MS Paint, PS for the finishing touches 
Time: weeks!
